Jobly is an AI-powered job search app designed to make finding and applying for jobs faster, easier, and more effective. Instead of just showing job listings like platforms such as LinkedIn or Indeed, Jobly helps users through the entire application process.

Users create a profile with their qualifications, skills, location, and graduation year. The app’s AI then recommends jobs that closely match their experience and career goals, helping them focus on the most relevant opportunities.

What makes Jobly different is its built-in AI support. When users select a job, the app researches the company and uses that information to generate tailored cover letters and job-specific CVs. It also includes an application tracker so users can keep track of every role they apply for in one place, making the whole job search process more organized and less stressful.
